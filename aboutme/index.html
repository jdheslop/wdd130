About Me

Jackson Heslop

Hello everyone. My name is Jackson. I'm originally from Wallsburg, Utah, but have lived in Germany for almost 20 years. I served a mission in Oklahoma City and, after studying at UVSC, I got a job working for a German company which manufactures machinery used in the pharmaceutical industry. This gave me the opportunity to transfer to Germany and I have been here ever since.

I'm happily married and have four children. Our oldest is currently serving in the Berlin, Germany mission as a Persian speaking missionary.

Over the years, I've seen how important IT solutions are and so, as a hobby, I started working on the Web & Computer Programming certificate.

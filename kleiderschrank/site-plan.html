Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is for people who enjoy designing fashion as a fun game. First, a topic is selected - for example, "Lunch with the Queen". Then you will search through various images of shoes to find the pair of shoes that they would wear to that event.

Audience

My intitial intended audience is mostly teenager girls and women who are interested in fashion and enjoy creating fashion ideas to fit a purpose.

Home page

The Kleider Schrank
Define Your Style - A Fashion Creation for Every Situation

The Kleider Schrank. The Wardrobe.
The place where you keep the things that make you look your best. With the right wardrobe you can master every situation. And it all starts with the shoes.

Welcome to the ultimate fashion challenge! Are you ready to put your styling skills to the test? Our web site is the perfect place for all the fashion enthusiasts out there who love a good challenge. Get ready to pick the best shoes to wear for each theme. Whether you want to compete with friends or challenge yourself, our game is the perfect way to spend your free time. So, get your creativity flowing and start creating the most stunning looks. Let's see who has what it takes to become the ultimate fashionista! The Kleider Schrank is waiting for you to show the world your fashion expertise.

Design fashion by yourself is fun, but designing with friends is even funnier. The Kleider Schrank is the perfect party game where everyone gets to turn on their creative genius and design the best fashion for every situation. Whether you select a challenging theme or a light-hearted one for a lot of laughs, the theme enables you to view the shoes with a new and fresh perspective. The virtual window-shopping experience gives you the opportunities to look at fashion in a way that you never would have before.

In order to find the best shoes for every situation, you need a large selection to choose from. As the website is currently being developed, the main selection focuses on Sneakers and Formal Shoes - but don't worry. More shoes and more functionality will be coming soon. We understand that even if you find the correct style, if the color isn't right then nothing is right. But this is also not a cause to worry. More colors will be added in the future as well.

Whether it is something from your everyday life, the fulfilment of a dream, or something that would only happen in a fantasy world, selecting the theme is the basis for directly your thoughts and creative juices in the right direction.

Then, Start Filling Your Wardrobe

Browse through foot apparel options and add everything that you think matches your them and looks good to your Wardrobe. Right now, this website has two types of shoes to select from: Sneakers and Formal Shoes. There is one page dedicated to each of these types of shoes, but don't worry - more options will be coming in the future!

Finally, Select the Perfect Shoes

Once you have filled your Wardrobe, you still need to find the perfect pair of shoes. Additional pages for both the Wardrobe and to select the perfect shoes will be added in the future.
